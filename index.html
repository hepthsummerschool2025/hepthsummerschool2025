About the summer school

This summer school is a continuation of two series of summer schools hosted by the Kavli Institute for Theoretical Sciences in Beijing (2021 in Liyang, 2023 in Shanghai, and 2024 in Beijing) and by the Yau Center in Southeast University (2020 online, 2021 online, 2023 in Nanjing and 2024 in Nanjing). We plan to merge these two series and hold the summer school annually in China, starting with this first edition in Hangzhou in 2025. The goal of this series of summer schools is to expose students and postdocs in China to the frontiers in theoretical high energy physics and introducing to them the basic research techniques in these subfields.





Time and venue

The summer school is tentatively planned to take place in Hangzhou from Aug. 4 to Aug. 15, 2025.
